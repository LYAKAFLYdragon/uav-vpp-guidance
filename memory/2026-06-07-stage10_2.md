### Key Insight
The corrected benchmark reveals a **geometry-dependent transfer gap**, not a universal failure:
- **Head-on geometries transfer perfectly** (100%) from simple backend to JSBSim F-16.
- **Crossing geometries fail completely** (0%) on JSBSim, even though they achieve ~75% on the simple backend.

The crossing failures are `out_of_bounds` at ~160-170 steps with min_range ~740-840 m (close to success zone but then diverge). This suggests the F-16's turn rate/energy limits prevent successful lead-turn completion in crossing geometries, whereas the simple point-mass model has no such constraints.
